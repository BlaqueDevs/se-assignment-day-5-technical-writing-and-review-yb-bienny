Understanding your audience's expertise level is important as it helps one to understand their expectations and realize what the audience aims at gaining from the information presented which helps one to tailor their communication strategies. For example, for regular folks one is compelled to use plain language and employ analogies and relevant examples while for expets one should utilise precise terminology, provide concise detailed information and build upon the audience's existing understanding.
It also helps to structure the content effectively through use of clear formatting.
